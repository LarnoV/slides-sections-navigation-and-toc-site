Help / FAQ

How do I create sections?

Use Tag mode and add {section: Name} via the sidebar’s Bulk-tag selected, or switch to Layout mode and apply your section-header layout.

Thumbnails don't load

Ensure your network allows slides.googleapis.com and your admin allows Marketplace add-ons.

Bulk-tag does nothing

Make sure you’re in Tag mode (use Change mode in the sidebar) and that slides are selected in the filmstrip.

Rebuild the TOC

Click Build / Update TOC in the sidebar. The first slide becomes your TOC with links to section headers.
